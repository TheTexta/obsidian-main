#calculus 

$\text{Say we have }$
$\sum_{n=k}^{\infty}a_n$
$\text{ where }$
$a_n=(-1)^n\cdot b_n$
$\text{ or }$
$a_n=(-1)^{n+1}\cdot b_n$
$\text{ where }$
$\text { 1. }b_n\geq0$
$\text{ 2. } \lim_{n->\infty}{b_n}=0$
$\text{ 3. } a_n \text{ is a decreasing sequence.}$
$\text{Then } \sum_{n=k}^{\infty}a_n \text{ is a converging sequence}$
