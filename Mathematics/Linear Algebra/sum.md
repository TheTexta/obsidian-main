Sum formula for matrix multiplication

$(AB)_{ij}=(\text{row i of A})\cdot (\text{column j of B})$

$=(a_{i1},a_{i2},...,a_{in})\pmatrix{b_{1i}\\b_{2i}\\...\\b_{mi}}$
$$
(AB)_{ij}=\sum^n_{k=1}{a_{ik}b_{kj}}$$
