Some algebraic properties
Let $A,B,C$ be $m \times n$ matrices, $b,c \in \mathbb{R}$.
1. $A+B = B+A$
2. $c(A+B) = cA + cB$
3. $(b+c)A = bA + cA$
4. $A+B+C$ mean $(A+B) + C$ or $A+(B+C)$ which are equal anyway
